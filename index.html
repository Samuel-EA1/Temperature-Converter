Conversion Formulas

Celsius to Fahrenheit

° F = ° C × (9/5) + 32


Celsius to Kelvin

K = ° Celsius + 273.15

Fahrenheit to Celsius

° C = ° F - 32 × (5/9)


Fahrenheit to Kelvin

K = (F - 32) × (5/9) + 273.15

Kelvin to Fahrenheit

F = (K - 273.15) × 9/5 + 32


Kelvin to Celsius

° C = K - 273.15
